About ThIRED

ThIRED is the first collection of thermal IR images with pixel-level edge annotations. This dataset consists of 20 thermal infrared images (1024 × 768 pixels), selected from the multi-scenario multi-modality dataset, also known as M3FD*.

To ensure high-quality evaluation and training, pixel-accurate ground truth edge maps were generated through a rigorous manual annotation process using the LabelBox platform. Unlike automated edge generation, this detailed human supervision ensures that the ground truth preserves the full structural integrity of the thermal scene. On average, each image contains approximately 900 edges, annotated as polylines, resulting in a total of around 18,000 annotated edges.

* Liu et al., “Target-aware dual adversarial learning and a multi-scenario multi-modality benchmark to fuse infrared and visible for object detection,” CVPR 2022.
